Connect a pro camera (Sony a7S III, DJI Osmo Pocket 3…)
  • DJI Osmo Pocket 3: plug into this device over USB-C and switch the camera to Webcam Mode. It appears in the camera menu above — no drivers.
  • Sony a7S III / a7 IV / FX3: Setup → USB → USB Streaming, then connect over USB. The camera shows up as a video source.
  • Anything with HDMI out: a small HDMI→USB capture stick makes any cinema camera appear as a source here.
  • Then pick it in the camera menu that appears at the bottom-left of the viewfinder.
