When to shoot

Phone camera, or a pro camera over USB. Frames go to the server for analysis only — nothing renders until you ask.

Connect a pro camera (Sony a7S III, DJI Osmo Pocket 3…)
  • DJI Osmo Pocket 3: plug in over USB-C, switch to Webcam Mode. It appears in the lens menu.
  • Sony a7S III / a7 IV / FX3: Setup → USB → USB Streaming, connect over USB.
  • Anything with HDMI out: an HDMI→USB capture stick makes it a source here.
