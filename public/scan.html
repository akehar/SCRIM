Walk your set before the shoot.

Hit SCAN THIS ROOM and walk the space slowly — the phone’s LiDAR builds a 3D scene right here, then the day’s sun path draws through it.

Import a Gaussian-splat scan of any location and orbit it with the day’s sun path drawn through the space.

  1. Scan the space with Scaniverse (free, App Store) — a slow walk around does it.
  2. In Scaniverse: Share → Export model → Splat (.splat) → Save to Files.
  3. Come back and hit IMPORT SCAN below.
